    About me

    Hi! My name is Felipe, I am a developer focused on information security and I live in Santo André - São Paulo.

    Technical training in mechatronics, during this period I created and participated in several projects, many of them from Arduino to Assembly. I started the database technology college at Fatec São José dos Campos, but I am currently studying Information Security at Fatec São Caetano do Sul College. I have knowledge in agile methodologies, file versioning (GIT), creation and maintenance of databases , networks, project management, programming (Python, Java, C), DevOps, HTML, CSS. I won the Young Leadership Award from the State of São Paulo in 2016, for promoting and developing projects that added value to students and teachers at the educational institution where I carried out my technical training in mechatronics.

    My Vision: Technology is a powerful tool! And my goal is: to solve as many problems as possible with her help and in partnership with a team willing to make a difference!
